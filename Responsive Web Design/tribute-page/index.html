Batman

the dark knight

Here is a timeline of the life of batman the dark knight:

  • 1966 - The first Batman movie hit theaters. Adam West and Burt Ward immortalized their performances in the roles of Batman and Robin, respectively. Another great highlight was the work of César Romero, as one of the Jokers that would go down in cinema history. The plot of the film follows the duo of heroes trying to save Commodore Schmidlapp, who is in danger on board a yacht.

  • 1989-This was the first production that belonged to a cinematographic universe, Batman had the remarkable direction of Tim Burton and brought Michael Keaton in the main role. With Jack Nicholson making history with his portrayal of the villainous Joker, the film showcased the character's transformation into the criminal clown.

  • 1992-Counting once again with Michael Keaton in the lead role and Tim Burton directing, Batman: Returns continued the story we saw in the previous feature film, but this time Bruce Wayne needs to face the dangerous Penguin.

  • 1997-The fourth and final film in The Batman Motion Picture anthology, Batman & Robin featured the return of Chris O'Donnell as Batman's partner, now brought to life by George Cloney. The plot follows Batman, Robin, and newcomer Batgirl battling Mr. Freeze, Poison Ivy, and Bane, who have teamed up in an attempt to take over Gotham.

  • 2005-Kicking off the franchise that would take the Batman movies to a level never before achieved, Batman Begins brought Christian Bale shining as Batman. The feature film rebooted the character's story once again, again showing Bruce Wayne's childhood, the murder of his parents, the training he underwent, and his recruitment into the League of Shadows.

  • 2008-Considered to this day the best Batman movie ever produced, The Dark Knight made history by receiving 8 Oscar nominations, having won two: Best Sound Editing and Best Supporting Actor. The latter was the posthumous award won by Heath Ledger, for his incredible portrayal of the villainous Joker.
